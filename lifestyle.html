The Lifestyle

It's your time

Picture yourself sitting on the wide-open patio enjoying your morning coffee and watching pink and grey galahs fly overhead.

This is a place of peace in the quiet countryside, a long way from the hustle and bustle of suburbia.

Here you will find relaxation, time and space to just be you.

It's your place

Beautiful gardens surround this home on two sides. Bright roses and hardy natives combine to bring colour and cheer to spring, and green freshness all year around. A perfect garden for an owner to lovingly maintain or for a garden-lover to mold into their own dream.

It's your retreat

Enjoy your time on your terms. Mornings happily pottering in the workshop or garden, lazy afternoons picnicking in your own back yard. Grab your drink of choice and enjoy cherry blossoms and grapevines only 30 metres from your back door. Relaxation is here.

It's your choice

Plump pumpkins or crispy kale? Juicy apples or ripe apricots? Every season will yield fresh delights to your table top with the conveniently raised vegetable beds and abundant fruit trees.

The hard part is choosing what to pick. The easy part is creating something wonderful in your bright, cheery kitchen.

It's your back yard

Enjoy a lazy Sunday walking through the local bush, being surprised by a mother kangaroo with her joey or taking in nature's display of wildflowers.

It's your time to play

Whether its the grandkids on bikes, or a bonfire after dinner, there is so much space for that outdoor lifestyle you love.

Pitch a tent with the grandkids for fun or invite friends for a weekend at your place in the country.

It's your home

Here you have found relaxation, time and space to just be you. So relax. Enjoy. Be.

The tree-change lifestyle awaits you in this friendly and active community, 95km east of Perth city, WA.

This property beckons with a whisper, entreating you to come, sit, relax, and start living the life you've always dreamed of.
