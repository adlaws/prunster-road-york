The Property

Space

The bright, airy and welcoming home at 36 Prunster road sits on a 5 acre block of meadow, fruit trees and a few gum trees. It boasts a huge garage/workshop/gym which will make any man’s heart sing, and a cozy office/reading nook to comfort any book-lover. With 4 huge bedrooms (in addition to the study) there is plenty of room for crafts, hobbies and guests.

Nature

The neighbours are friendly, as are the sheep and galahs. The ducks and chickens peck amidst the raised vegetable gardens and the huge rainwater tank keeps the vegetables, fruit trees and native plant gardens looking fresh year-round. The lower meadow is perfect for that mid-winter bonfire with friends and family, and welcomes tents if the house gets to over-flowing.

Entertain

The huge kitchen-dining-lounge is perfect for entertaining and flows naturally out onto the large patio and grassed area. The verandah and paving circles the whole house, which is essential for kids who love trikes and scooters and trucks and prams.

Family and Friends

The land has plenty of space for horses, or kids on bikes, and if you’ve always dreamed of a ride-on mower and a straw hat in the morning, and a beer or wine in the shade in the afternoons, look no further.
