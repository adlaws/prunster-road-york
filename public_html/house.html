The Property

Exterior

Welcome to 36 Prunster Road, York, Western Australia. This spacious home sits cheerfully on its 5 acre block surrounded by rolling hills and peaceful meadows.

The neighbours are friendly, as are the ducks and chickens.

The fruit trees, gum trees and grape vines grow happily, enjoying the rain over winter and water from the huge rainwater tank in summer months.

The massive garage/gym/workshop is a key feature of this property. Measuring 12m x 9m it has abundant space for vehicles, machinery, tools and gym equipment. It has double garage doors on the north face and a glass sliding door on the south face.

Kitchen

This kitchen was built to be beautiful and convenient with its walk-in pantry, huge fridge-freezer recess, 5 burner cook top and dishwasher. It is spacious and bright with meadow views.

Dining

In true country style, this dining room is an extension of the kitchen, the very heart of the home. With warm fires in winter and peaceful sunrises in summer, it is a favourite place to eat, chat and relax.

Main Lounge

Big, bright and welcoming, the lounge encourages you to kick back, relax and enjoy the view. There is room for the whole family, and plenty of space for entertaining.

Master Bedroom

Spacious, yet surprisingly cosy, this room entreats the occupant to relax and sleep well after a full day of country air.

Master Bedroom Ensuite

Accessible through the double walk-in-robe, this is a crisp, bright ensuite.

Formal Lounge

A favourite room in this house, the formal lounge is inviting, comfortable and air conditioned. It is perfectly suited to evening drinks with relaxing music and a great book.

Study

Currently a book-lover’s paradise, this room is ideal as an office, craft or hobby room, or a 5th bedroom.

Bedrooms 2, 3 & 4

These large double bedrooms all have built in robes and ceiling fans. They are perfect for hosting guests and grandkids, or even additional craft and hobby rooms.

Main Bathroom

Claw-footed bathtub. Need I say more?

Laundry

Conveniently designed with direct access to the back yard, this room is for dirty work boots and gardening gloves, and the occasional load of washing. With one full wall of cupboards with sliding doors, the laundry provides massive storage space.

Located between the main bathroom and the laundry is a separate toilet, not pictured.

Patio

This well-loved patio has seen long conversations, sunsets over hills and many a happy barbeque.

This property beckons with a whisper, entreating you to come, sit, relax, and start living the life you've always dreamed of.
