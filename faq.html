In a Nutshell

This 5 acre (2 hectare) block nestles snugly in the Avon Valley, under the shadow of Mt Bakewell, so if you are looking for a peaceful location to get away from the suburban sprawl, then look no further. Featuring four bedrooms, two bathrooms, a study (with NBN connection), lounge and large family room, this house has great views and ample bonus features that make it a perfect spot for your family and friends to enjoy a country lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Large open plan kitchen/dining/family area which opens out onto the alfresco area
  • Kitchen with lots of storage space, bench space and walk-in pantry
  • Fully tiled floor in hall/ kitchen/dining/family rooms, with carpet in the lounge and master bedroom and floating floorboards in the remaining bedrooms.
  • Reverse cycle air conditioning in the lounge room. All other areas have fans
  • Master bedroom with walk-through-robe and en-suite
  • Queen sized minor bedrooms
  • 12m x 9m powered garage/workshop/shed.
  • a solar hot water system
  • fruit trees/ grape vines/four adjoining poultry enclosures and two 18 sq metre vegetable gardens
  • approximately one third of the block is fenced off with both scheme and tank water on tap, and is suitable for horses, sheep or cattle

The township of York is located just 100 km east of Perth. It has a public hospital, ample medical facilities, a district high school as well as school bus services to Northam Senior High School.
